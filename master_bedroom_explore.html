MASTER BEDROOMS

The master bedrooms at Ayi’s Hotel are designed for luxury,
comfort, and relaxation. Each room is elegantly furnished with premium bedding,
stylish décor, and large windows offering breathtaking views. Guests can enjoy modern amenities,
including a smart TV, high-speed WiFi, automated lighting controls, and a spacious work desk.
The attached en-suite bathroom features a rainfall shower, high-quality toiletries, and plush towels,
ensuring a rejuvenating experience. Whether traveling for business or leisure, our master bedrooms provide a tranquil escape.
