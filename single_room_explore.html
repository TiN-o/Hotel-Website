SINGLE ROOMS

Whether you're in town for business, a quiet getaway, or just passing through, our single rooms are
thoughtfully designed to give solo travelers the perfect balance of comfort, style, and functionality.
Each room is fully equipped with a cozy bed, an elegant workspace, free high-speed Wi-Fi, and a sleek en-suite
bathroom—everything you need to unwind and recharge. At Ayi’s Hotel, we believe small can still feel grand.
Expect smart storage solutions, ambient lighting, and charming décor that reflects our signature blend of warmth
and modern flair. Wake up refreshed and ready to explore the vibrant surroundings or tackle the day’s
agenda—all from your serene sanctuary.
