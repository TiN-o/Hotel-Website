DUAL ROOMS

Our dual rooms are ideal for friends, colleagues, and couples seeking
a shared yet comfortable space. Designed to offer both privacy and convenience,
each room features two premium-quality beds, a cozy seating area, high-speed internet,
and ample storage. The rooms also include temperature control settings,
bedside charging stations, and a well-lit workstation for productivity.
Perfect for guests looking for affordability without compromising quality,
these rooms provide an inviting and restful atmosphere.
